# Subscription App Profile

Use this profile for apps with subscriptions, trials, paywalls, or entitlement checks.

## Extra Focus

- Restore purchases
- Entitlement validation
- Paywall claims
- Trial and renewal copy
- App Store and Play billing compliance
- Cancellation and account deletion paths

## Default Risk Posture

Treat broken restore purchase and server/client entitlement mismatch as release blockers.
